Welcome

I am a Ph.D. graduate in Software Engineering and a researcher in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Optimization, with a focus on imbalanced data classification. My work involves designing algorithms for feature selection and representation learning in complex and high-dimensional datasets.

I have applied my research to real-world problems in healthcare and financial data analysis. I also have experience in teaching, supervising student projects, and reviewing scientific papers.

My main research interests include machine learning algorithms, deep learning (CNN), imbalanced data classification, and optimization techniques.
